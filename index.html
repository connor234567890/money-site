How to Choose a Gaming PC Under $1500

1) Prioritize the GPU

For gaming performance, the graphics card matters most. RTX 4070 is ideal for 1440p high/ultra settings. RTX 4060 Ti is excellent for high 1080p and good 1440p with tuned settings.

2) RAM: 16GB minimum, 32GB is a bonus

16GB is the minimum for modern gaming. 32GB helps with multitasking, streaming, and future-proofing.

3) Storage: NVMe SSD only

A 1TB NVMe SSD is the standard. If you hate uninstalling games, a 2TB NVMe SSD is worth paying for.

4) Check cooling & airflow

Multiple case fans and decent airflow help maintain performance and reduce noise.

Final Thoughts

If you want the best overall gaming experience under $1500, choose an RTX 4070 build. If you want strong value or extra storage, an RTX 4060 Ti system can still deliver excellent gaming performance.
