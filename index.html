How to Choose a Gaming PC Under $1500

1) Prioritize the GPU

For gaming performance, the graphics card matters most. RTX 4070 is ideal for 1440p high/ultra settings. RTX 4060 Ti is excellent for 1080p and good 1440p with tuned settings (DLSS helps).

2) RAM: 16GB minimum, 32GB is a bonus

16GB is the minimum for modern gaming. 32GB helps with multitasking, streaming, and future-proofing.

3) Storage: NVMe SSD only

A 1TB NVMe SSD is the standard. If you hate uninstalling games, a 2TB NVMe SSD is worth it.

4) Cooling & airflow

Multiple case fans and decent airflow help maintain performance and reduce noise.

Final Thoughts

If you want the best all-around gaming experience under $1500, go RTX 4070. If you want value or extra storage, the RTX 4060 Ti picks can be a smart buy.
