How to Choose a Gaming PC Under $1500

1. Focus on the GPU First

For gaming performance, the graphics card matters most. An RTX 4070 is ideal for high-settings 1440p gaming and future-proofing.

2. Make Sure You Get At Least 16GB RAM

Modern games benefit from 16GB minimum. 32GB provides better multitasking and long-term usability.

3. Don’t Ignore Cooling & Airflow

Look for multiple fans and proper case ventilation. Overheating kills performance.

4. Storage Should Be NVMe SSD

Avoid slow HDD-only builds. 1TB NVMe SSD is the sweet spot for game installs.
