How to Choose a Gaming PC Under $1500

1) Prioritize the GPU

For gaming performance, the graphics card matters most. An RTX 4070 delivers strong high-settings 1440p performance. RTX 4060 Ti is excellent for 1080p and good 1440p.

2) RAM & Memory

16GB is the minimum for modern gaming. More RAM helps with multitasking and future games.

3) Storage Matters

A 2TB NVMe SSD gives you room for many games without uninstalling. SSD speed helps load times too.

4) Cooling & Airflow

Good case airflow prevents overheating and keeps fans quieter under load.

Final Thoughts

If you want the best all-around gaming experience under $1500, the RTX 4070 build wins. If you want storage or value, the other picks offer solid alternatives.
