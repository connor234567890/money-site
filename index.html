How to Choose a Gaming PC Under $1500

1. Prioritize the GPU

For gaming performance, the graphics card matters most. RTX 4070 is ideal for 1440p ultra settings, while RTX 4060 Ti is strong for high 1080p and entry-level 1440p.

2. Make Sure You Get 16GB+ RAM

16GB is the minimum. 32GB gives better long-term performance and multitasking.

3. Use NVMe SSD Storage

Avoid HDD-only builds. A 1TB NVMe SSD ensures fast load times.

Final Thoughts

If you want maximum performance under $1500, RTX 4070 builds are the sweet spot. For better value, an RTX 4060 Ti system still delivers excellent gaming performance at a lower price.
